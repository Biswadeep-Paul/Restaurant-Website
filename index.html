Popular Items We Serve

Chicken Briyani

Savor the aromatic delight of our signature Chicken Biryani at Urban Delishy. Crafted with tender chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices, each bite offers a symphony of flavors. Slow-cooked to perfection, it's a culinary masterpiece that promises an unforgettable experience.

Butter Chicken

Indulge in the creamy richness of our Butter Chicken, a beloved favorite at Urban Delishy. Succulent pieces of chicken are simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy, infused with aromatic spices and finished with a touch of cream. This classic dish is a celebration of flavors.

Reshmi Kabab

Delight in the succulent goodness of our Reshmi Kabab, a culinary treasure at Urban Delishy . Tender, marinated chicken pieces are skewered and grilled to perfection, resulting in a melt-in-your-mouth experience. Infused with aromatic spices and served with a tangy mint chutney.

Chicken 65

Experience the fiery delight of our Chicken 65, a tantalizing favorite at Restaurant. Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, each bite-sized piece of chicken is coated in a spicy marinade bursting with flavor. Served hot and garnished with curry leaves, it's a zesty treat that ignites.
